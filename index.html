Nemisindo Demos

Procedural Audio Generation

Nemisindo presents a collection of games that showcase the power of procedural audio generation. All of this demos were build in Scratch and are free to play. All the procedural sounds were produced by Nemisindo.

Procedural Audio: Pros and Cons

Pros Cons
Less storage space needed (audio is generated in real-time) More CPU usage compared to pre-recorded sounds
Highly adaptive to gameplay or environment changes Can be harder to control or fine-tune exactly
Unlimited sound variations Requires more complex development skills
